Vlads Van Vault is a small and devoted small business dedicated to helping you get
there. We opened shop in 1969 and rented three single vans, today, just thirty years later,
we have grown to over 50 vans and have been stimulating the local economy all the way. We
are proud to be based in Las Vegas Nevada and hope that we can be around to stimulate the
local economy for years to come. The customers would most likely to know about the origins
of the company and about our ceo.
